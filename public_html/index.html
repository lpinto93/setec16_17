For this project we will develop a service capable of receiving and storing data from the SDR system. The input will be processed and visualized in numerous ways, such as heatmaps and statistics, which will be made available to the general public through a web interface. Our objective is also to provide an interface with the AZGO application by processing and relaying its requests as well as providing it with the desired information. On the other hand, we will interact with the SDR group by requesting data and transmitting orders.

Know

We process all the data and give it back to you in a clean and interesting way. Want to know how many people were in FEUP last week? How about the most popular spots for meeting people? You can know this and more in our Statistics page.

Visualize

Check out our Heatmaps section to see where people were in FEUP - at any time you want. Generate your own heatmap with a simple click.

Find Your Way

With our mobile application, you can know where you are -- and how to get to where you want to be! Meet your friends in FEUP in the simplest way ever.
